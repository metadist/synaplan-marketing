Why lock yourself in?

The AI landscape changes every week. Today's best model is tomorrow's legacy. Synaplan gives you the freedom to switch providers instantly, without rewriting a single line of code.

Your Benefits

  • Freedom of Choice: Use OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Mistral, or local models via Ollama.
  • Cost Optimization: Route simple queries to cheaper models and complex ones to flagship models.
  • Data Privacy: Keep sensitive data on local models or trusted providers.
  • Future Proofing: Adopt new models the day they are released.

How it works

Synaplan abstracts the AI provider layer. You configure your preferred models in the admin panel, and the platform handles the rest. Switching from GPT-4 to Claude 3.5 Sonnet takes seconds, not weeks.
