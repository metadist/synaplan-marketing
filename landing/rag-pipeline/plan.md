# Campaign Plan: RAG Pipeline

**Goal**: Promote Synaplan's Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) capabilities.

**Target Audience**:
*   Knowledge Managers
*   Enterprise Architects
*   Legal Teams
*   Researchers
*   Anyone needing to chat with their documents.

**Value Proposition**:
*   **Accuracy**: Ground AI answers in your own data (PDFs, Docs, Websites).
*   **Privacy**: Keep sensitive documents secure, process locally or via trusted providers.
*   **Ease of Use**: Upload documents and start chatting instantly. No complex setup.
*   **Multi-Modal**: Chat with images, charts, and text.

**Key Messaging**:
*   "Chat with your documents. Securely."
*   "Turn your knowledge base into an AI assistant."
*   "Stop hallucinations. Start retrieving facts."
*   "Synaplan RAG: The bridge between your data and AI."
