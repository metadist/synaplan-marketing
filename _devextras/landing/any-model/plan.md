# Campaign Plan: Any Model

**Goal**: Promote Synaplan's ability to switch AI models on the fly.

**Target Audience**:
*   AI Developers
*   Data Scientists
*   Tech-savvy Business Users
*   Companies looking for flexibility and avoiding vendor lock-in.

**Value Proposition**:
*   **Freedom**: Switch between OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Mistral, etc. instantly.
*   **Cost Control**: Use cheaper models for simple tasks, powerful models for complex ones.
*   **Privacy**: Choose local models or specific providers based on data sensitivity.
*   **Future-Proof**: Adapt to new models as they emerge without rewriting code.

**Key Messaging**:
*   "Change your AI model - while using it."
*   "Don't get locked in."
*   "The right model for the right task."
*   "Synaplan: The AI Model Agnostic Platform."

**Languages**:
*   English (`en`)
*   German (`de`) - *Planned*
